New Science Building Opens Next Week

Featured Academic March 25, 2025
Admin

The University is proud to announce the grand opening of our new state-of-the-art Science Building. After three years of construction and a $45 million investment, the facility will officially open its doors on Monday, April 7, 2025.

The 120,000 square foot building features:

  • 24 advanced research laboratories
  • 6 teaching laboratories
  • A 200-seat lecture hall with the latest audio-visual technology
  • Study spaces designed to promote collaboration
  • Energy-efficient systems that make it a LEED Platinum certified building

"This facility represents our commitment to advancing scientific research and providing our students with the best possible learning environment," said University President Dr. Alicia Chen. "The building will serve as a hub for innovation and discovery for generations to come."

The grand opening ceremony will take place at 10 AM on Monday and will feature remarks from President Chen, the Dean of Sciences, and state officials who helped fund the project. Tours of the building will be available throughout the day.

All students and faculty are invited to attend. The event will also be livestreamed on the university website for those unable to attend in person.
